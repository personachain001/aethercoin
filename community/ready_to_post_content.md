## Reddit 首帖

**标题:** [Official] Introducing AetherCoin (AETH) - A Fair Cryptocurrency Where Everyone Can Mine

**内容:**

Hello r/CryptoCurrency! 👋

I'm excited to introduce **AetherCoin (AETH)** - a cryptocurrency built from the ground up to solve mining centralization.

## The Problem

As we all know, Bitcoin mining has become increasingly centralized. ASIC manufacturers and large mining pools control the vast majority of hashrate. Regular people with consumer hardware are effectively locked out.

## Our Solution

AetherCoin uses a **RandomX-based Proof of Work** algorithm that is:

- **ASIC-resistant**: Memory-hard algorithm that makes specialized hardware unprofitable
- **GPU-friendly**: Your gaming graphics card can mine efficiently
- **CPU-minable**: Even laptop CPUs can participate meaningfully
- **Fair launched**: Zero pre-mine, zero ICO. Every token earned through work.

## Key Specs

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Algorithm | RandomX (RPW) |
| Block Time | 10 minutes |
| Initial Reward | 50 AETH |
| Halving | Every 210,000 blocks |
| Total Supply | 21,000,000 AETH |
| Smart Contracts | EVM Compatible |

## What Makes Us Different

1. **No pre-mine whatsoever** - The founders mine just like everyone else
2. **Dynamic difficulty** - Adjusts automatically to maintain consistent block times
3. **Optional privacy** - Choose between transparent and private transactions
4. **Community governance** - All protocol upgrades decided by community vote

## Get Involved

- **GitHub**: github.com/aethercoin/aethercoin (full source code available)
- **Discord**: discord.gg/aethercoin
- **Twitter**: @AetherCoin

We believe blockchain was supposed to be democratizing technology. AetherCoin is our attempt to bring that vision back.

**Ask us anything in the comments!** 🚀
